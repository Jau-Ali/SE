PRIVACY POLICY

 

At ParkIt, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy when you engage in any contests, sweepstakes, or promotions offered through our platform. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information during these activities.

 

Information Collection

When you participate in a Promotion, we may collect certain personal information, including but not limited to:

  • 1.) Contact Information: This may include your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number.
  • 2.) Eligibility Information: To verify your eligibility to participate, we may collect your age, date of birth, or other relevant details
  • 3.) Entry Details: Information related to your entry into the Promotion, such as your responses, submissions, or entries.

    •  

    Use of Information

    We use the personal information collected during Promotions for the following purposes:

  • 1.) Notification: To notify winners and distribute prizes or rewards.
  • 2.) Promotion Management: To manage and administer the Promotion, including verifying eligibility and compliance with the Promotion rules.
  • 3.) Marketing Communication: With your consent, we may use your contact information to send you marketing communications about our products, services, or upcoming Promotions. You can opt out of these communications at any time
  • 4.) Legal Compliance: To comply with legal requirements, including tax reporting and auditing.

    •  

    Sharing of Information

    We may share your personal information with the following entities:

  • 1.) Sponsors: If a Promotion is sponsored by a third party, we may share your information with the sponsor for prize fulfillment and other related purposes.
  • 2.) Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist with Promotion management, including prize distribution and email communications. These service providers are bound by contractual agreements to protect your information.

    •  

    Promotion Rules

    Please note that Promotions may have specific rules governing the collection and use of personal information. In case of any conflict between these rules and our general Privacy Policy, the rules of the Promotion will prevail.

     

    Security

    We take appropriate measures to safeguard your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments

     

    Your Choices

    You have the right to:

  • 1.) Review and update your personal information.
  • 2.) Opt out of receiving marketing communications.
  • 3.) Withdraw consent for the collection and use of your personal information.

    •  

    Changes to this Privacy Policy

    We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and the date of the last update will be revised accordingly.

     

    By participating in our Promotions, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about our practices regarding Promotions.
